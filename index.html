    Sign up for serving a delicious meal to around 150 of our brothers and sisters!

  • Teach A Class

  • Share Some Music

    We would love to host some live worship music! Come share your talents with those who are in need.

  • Be in Fellowship

    Set aside some time in your day to build relationships with others. Be a part of our community.

  • Educate Others

    SPread the word to your family and friends. Get your church and business involved today!

  • Donate

    Whether it be clothes, food or money. You can help by donating.
