Help Us Love Our Neighbors

The Law in God's Word is summed up in two commandments; love the Lord your God with all your hear, soul, mind and strenght, and love your neighbors as yourself. (Luke 10:27) Our ministry is a local outreach to our eighbors. We recognize the call to extend mercy to those who are lost. Our hope is that you would join us in the immense work here in Springfield and throughout Missouri. Here are a few ways you can help serve at the Veterans Coming Home Center.

Serve a Meal

If you are gifted in cooking for a large group of people, we have the facilities to help you reach the homeless. The best way to sign up for a meal is click here and follwo the Meal Train prompts. Serving a meal is an excellent way to bless our neighbors with sustenance they need.

Teach a Class

Do you desire to share some important knowledge or information you have? There are lots of different ways to share what you know. Click here to contact management to meet with and discuss ways you can teach others!
