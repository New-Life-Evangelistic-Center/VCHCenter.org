Serve a Meal

If you are gifted in cooking for a large group of people, we have the facilities to help you reach the homeless. The best way to sign up for a meal is click here and follow the Meal Train prompts. Serving a meal is an excellent way to bless our neighbors with sustenance they need.

Teach a Class

Do you desire to share some important knowledge or information you have? There are lots of different ways to share what you know.Click here to contact management to meet with and discuss ways you can teach others!

Share Some Music

Are you gifted with musical talent? There are many ways to share your talent with others. Schedule a time to worship in our center and spread teh joy of Christ through music.

Be In Fellowship

One of the best ways to help our neighbors is to build relationships with them. Our ministry is striving to build a healthy thriving Christian community at our shelter. Building long term relationships is hard, but so rewarding. Take some time throughout your week to come and visit!

Educate Others

Spread the mission of our ministry through your church and business. Word of mouth is the best way to get others involved. Feel free to reach out to management ways to spread the word.

Make a Donation

Giving to others is one of the most rewarding services we can provide.

There is always a great financial need as well. Giving even $15 a month makes a huge difference! All donations stay local as we seek the homeless in Springfield. You can drop off donations at 806 N. Jefferson Ave. Springfield, MO. As a non-profit 501(c)3 all donations are tax deductible.
